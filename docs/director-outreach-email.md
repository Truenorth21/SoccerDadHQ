# Director outreach — email templates

Goal: get club/school/coach decision-makers to **claim their profile** (revenue +
they bring their parent base, who then sign up for tryout alerts).

Keep it short, personal, and value-first. Send from a real person, not "noreply@".
Replace `{...}` placeholders. Link straight to **their profile**, not the homepage.

---

## Cold email (primary)

**Subject options (pick one, keep it specific):**
- `{ClubName} is on SoccerDadHQ — want to manage your page?`
- `Parents are comparing {ClubName} on SoccerDadHQ`
- `Your {ClubName} profile + this month's ranking`

**Body:**

> Hi {FirstName},
>
> I run **SoccerDadHQ** — a directory where Florida soccer families compare clubs,
> read real parent reviews, and look up tryouts. **{ClubName}** is already listed:
> {profileURL}
>
> Right now it's an auto-built page. If you **claim it**, you can:
> - Edit your info, leagues and upload your logo
> - **Post your tryout dates** — they show in our homepage ticker and the regional newsletter, right when parents are looking
> - Respond to parent reviews
> - See profile views and your community ranking, month over month
>
> {OPTIONAL_RANK_LINE}
>
> Claiming takes a couple minutes here: **soccerdadhq.com/claim**
> (The listing stays free either way — claiming just lets you manage it.)
>
> Happy to send a 60-second walkthrough if that's easier.
>
> — {YourName}
> SoccerDadHQ · soccerdadhq.com

**`{OPTIONAL_RANK_LINE}`** — include only if they're ranked, e.g.:
> You're currently **#{N} among {Region} clubs** this month — families are voting, and you can rally yours from your dashboard.

---

## Follow-up (3–5 days later, if no reply)

**Subject:** `Re: {ClubName} on SoccerDadHQ`

> Hi {FirstName} — quick nudge in case this got buried. Tryout season is exactly
> when parents are searching, so a claimed page with your real dates gets seen.
> Your page: {profileURL} · Claim it: soccerdadhq.com/claim
>
> If you'd rather I just set it up and send you the login, reply "yes" and I'll handle it.
>
> — {YourName}

---

## Tips
- **Personalize the first line** with something real about the club (region, a recent result, their league). Generic = ignored.
- **Send Tue–Thu mornings.** Avoid Mondays/weekends.
- **One ask.** Claim. Don't also pitch ads in the same email.
- **Batch by region** so you can reference the regional newsletter/ranking.
- Track who you've contacted (a simple sheet: club, name, email, date, status).
